Description

A natural daily cleansing balm using plant extracts, that simply melts into the skin easily, dissolving dead skin cells, removing impurities and make up to reveal soft, supple, glowing skin. We have included 100% natural ECOCERT certified oat Silk in our Aromatic Cleansing Balm and Unscented Cleansing Balm. Oat Silk extract is a natural skin cleanser which removes dirt and oil from the skin; diminishes the pores, effectively fights dull skin, lightening and improving tone, texture and smoothness of the skin; provides a luxuriant feel with natural lipid and antioxidant content; having a brightening and mattifying effect on the skin. Oat Silk contains natural antioxidants, including avenathramides and vitamin E, with 7% lipid oat oil, 1% beta glucan and 17% proteins. Together they assist skin Health. Shea Butter to nourish, Watermelon Seed oil - helping replace essential skin lipids without blocking pores and helping to restore the skins natural elasticity, Crambe Seed oil which closely mimics human sebum with a strong concentrations of intensely nourishing Omega 9 and antioxidant vitamin E. Skin is intensely cleansed, nourished and renewed for a glowing complexion, leaving it clean, soft and fragrant with aromas of geranium, lavender, patchouli and neroli. This silky smooth formula retains the skins balance, refining, and giving a daily boost to dull, dry and mature skin but can be used morning and nigh by all skin types. Also available unscented.

How to Use

  • 1. Use clean, dry hands or spatula to scoop out a fingertip size of balm.
  • 2. Massage onto dry skin and feel the balm begin to melt and dissolve make-up, grime and pollutants.
  • 3. Add lukewarm water to emulsify the oil and activate a balm-to-milk deep cleansing experience. 
  • 4. Rinse off or for best results wipe clean with a dampened, lukewarm Bamboo Face Cleansing Cloth. Bamboo face cloths are antibacterial and antifungal, great for those with blemish prone skin yet gentle enough for sensitive prone skin.
  • 5. Doubles as a nourishing moisturising leave on mask | Use 2-3 times a week as a  leave on mask for 5-10 minutes before rinsing. 
 

Storage

  •     Store below 20°C in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and high temperatures.
  •     Do not add water to jar.
  •     Due to the nature of the oil composition you may see texture changes when exposed to different higher temperatures. This does not affect the efficacy of the product in any way
