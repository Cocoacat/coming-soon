SKINCARE MADE TO ORDER

Together with you we want to protect our planet. Therefore, we strive to be sustainable by producing our skincare made-to-order. Thereby, we reduce waste and limit the resources that are needed.!

Unlike other commercial cosmetics companies, we create your products after you place your order.

A percentage of profits from the sales of all Not So Balmy products is donated to small grassroots organizations working in the areas of environmental conservation, animal welfare .

Environmental Responsibility

All of our products are 100% vegan, sustainable, and cruelty free. Handmade right here in North Yorkshire, our eco-friendly and recyclable packaging stays true to our commitment of preserving the natural beauty of the world and each other..

Recyclable Packaging

We recycles all boxes, plastic bags, bubble wrap and Styrofoam received from our ingredient suppliers. At NOT SO BALMY, form is equal to function. We strive to ensure our packaging is beautiful and sustainable wherever possible, including the use of recycled aluminum and glass jars.

Animal Friendly

We have never participated in the activity of animal testing on our ingredients and or finished products, nor do we do business with any supply chain that participated in the act of animal testing. We currently purchases food-grade ingredients that have been evaluated on human volunteers. Our policy clearly states that we will not conduct business with any supplier that participates or champions such activities..

Vegan Friendly

Vegan skincare is the best choice for the planet. It takes 12 times as much land, 13 times as much fossil fuel and 15 times as much water to produce the same amount of animal protein versus plant protein. Moreover, with a rise in topical and internal sensitivities to gluten, we offer products which are suitable for all.

Who We Are!

We are a tiny handmade company based in the North Yorkshire Country Side of Boroughbridge; overlooked by the peaceful waters of the River Niddan and surrounded by pretty villages, where the hills are gentle and the patchwork of wheat fields and working farmland is lined with hedgerows that teem with wildlife.

We actually make the creams ourselves!) We formulate and make our own skin creams by hand from scratch in small batches using the right amount of science.

We do not outsource to a factory, which is quite unusual in skin care manufacturing, but this ensures you the highest level of product quality and freshness.

We also, make sure all raw materials we use to manufacture our products are not tested on animals and are cruelty free, of course! And we would never sell any skin product to customers that we have not tested on our own skin first.

We are proud that our handmade Not So Balmy skin care range fully complies with all legislation governing the manufacture of cosmetics in the UK and Europe, through the process of being fully laboratory tested and certified.

About Johanna

I am a fully trained professional Holistic beauty therapist, Aromatherapist, Sports Massage Therapist, Reflexologist, Spiritual Healer, Natural Cosmetic Formulator and Skincare Specialist who likes to take a common sense approach to formulating cosmetics.

As a skincare specialist I carry out various electrical and holistic facials including the Blend Fascial Facial which is equivalent to a natural face lift.

The seed for Not So Balmy was planted when I wanted to know what cosmetic manufacturers were putting into the skin care products I was using on my clients.

Being a professional Holistic therapist and Aromatherapist and being interested in science, natural beauty and cosmetics I realised I could develop my own skin care line as for many years I had been formulating and developing with my mother natural skin care recipes as a hobby, as we both have an interest in natural skin care. From then on I have been hooked, and it has always been a passion.

Developing my own skin care line just made sense and seemed to be a natural progression for my business. So, hence Not So Balmy was born.

And of course i am an animal lover, a vegan and a concerned environmentalist.
